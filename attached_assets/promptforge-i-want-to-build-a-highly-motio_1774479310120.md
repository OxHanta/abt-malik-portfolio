3. Include a video playback component with controls for playback, pause, and volume adjustment.
4. Incorporate an image gallery component with lightbox functionality and navigation controls.
5. Provide a contact form with fields for name, email, message, and a submit button, sending notifications to the artist's email address.
6. Ensure responsive design and accessibility features, including ARIA attributes, semantic HTML, and high contrast mode.
